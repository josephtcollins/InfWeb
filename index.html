InfWeb

This is an Information Website I put together for a family member's piano business and art portfolio. I made variations to a gallery I found online (uses jquery) as well as some php code for sending comments to an email account. I used bootstrap as to format most of the pages. It works on mobile, tablet size, and large to xl computer screens. Joe Collins
